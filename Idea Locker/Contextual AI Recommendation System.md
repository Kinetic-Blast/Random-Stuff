Purpose:
Enhance an AI assistant so it can suggest products, services, or tools naturally as part of its answers, based on the user’s question, without feeling like traditional advertising.

Key Features:
Context-aware: Recommendations only appear when relevant to the user’s query.
Transparent: Clearly labeled as suggestions or solutions.
Personalized (optional): Can factor in platform, preferences, or constraints while respecting privacy.
Seamless integration: Suggestions appear inline, allowing users to interact (expand, compare, skip).

How it Works:
AI parses user query to detect intent and extract key entities.
Recommendation engine matches entities to a curated database of products or services.
AI presents solutions naturally within its response, optionally explaining why each is relevant.
Feedback loop refines future recommendations based on user interaction.

Outcome:
Users get actionable, relevant solutions in real-time, making recommendations feel like a natural part of the assistant instead of intrusive advertising.
